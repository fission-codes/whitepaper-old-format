---
title: High Level Architecture
---

The Asimov architecture is made up of into 6 modular components, which group loosely into 3 segments:

1. Unified Storage
  a. Files
  b. Database
2. Digital Scarcity
  a. Identity
  b. Assets
3. Portable Compute
  a. Private & Trusted
  b. Public & Trustless
